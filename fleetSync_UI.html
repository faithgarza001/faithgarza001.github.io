Interviews

During the ideation phase of the project, I conducted user interviews to build new personas and to inform the design. Together with the team, we prepared an interview script with 32 open-ended questions, focusing on our target audiencesâ€™ values, motivations, and daily routines. In 4 days, I recruited and interviewed 7 users remotely. We referenced the user interview findings throughout the entire design process.

  • What objectives influenced your questions?
  • How many users did you interview?
  • What were the main insights from the interviews?
  • How did you use your findings?

Personas

We wanted to form a deeper understanding of our users' goals, needs, experiences, and behaviors. So, we created 4 personas for each of our user segments. They were based on user interviews and surveys, and we kept updating them throughout the project as we gathered more data. We used these personas whenever we wanted to step out of ourselves and reconsider our initial ideas.

  • Why did you decide that you need personas?
  • What data did you use to build your personas?
  • What information did you specify for each persona?
  • How did the personas affect the design process?
  • At which stages of the design process did you reflect back on the personas?
